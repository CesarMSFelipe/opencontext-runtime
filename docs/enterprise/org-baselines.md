# Org Baselines

## Purpose
Org baselines are scaffolded checks for unsafe provider, tool, trace, cache, memory, prompt, and release policy changes.

## Current Status
Design and local scaffolds are present. Do not treat the project as a fully certified enterprise platform yet.
